My Final Project

This is my Final Project for my CSC 106 class. My partner and I have recreated the game Plinko using Javascript code! How to play: First click play, then youll be taken to the screen where you can pick your ball color. Choose the color you want to play with and then click play again. Then you can click anywhere at the top of the screen to drop a ball and see how much you can win!
